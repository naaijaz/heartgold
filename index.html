About us

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as Golden heart marine co. SL. Ltd. GOLDEN heart marine IS A REPUTABLE MARITIME COMPANY that provides services in the maritime industry as per above content in Sierra Leone and Sub region through our partners. We provide professional services to facilitate all parties involved in cargo import and export operations.

We implement international industry standards in manning vessels, barges, and tugboats. We do provide crew to various offshore companies. We also have professionals outside Sierra Leone that prepare port master plan. Transshipment, and port/ terminal construction are our major areas of specialization. Barges, tugboats, and dredgers are within our reach. We are the only marine company in Sierra Leone and sub region that provides such services. Golden heart Marine, a maritime logistic company that adopted the one stop shopping system, where in you will find all your maritime needs. Transshipment with major mining companies engaged in the activities of mining and exporting Iron Ore and bauxite. Port and terminal construction via our joint venture partners.

Our dedicated teamwork around the clock to deliver operational excellence and provide viable solutions to our local and international clients.
